Info-CSD

CSD (Computer Science Department) has been operating since 1984, being chronologically the second Department
of Computers / Informatics in Greece. It belongs to the Faculty of Sciences and Technological Sciences of the University
of Crete, which operates in Heraklion. is one of the best Computer Science Departments in the European Union. It has
skilled professors with a good background since most of them they had completed their studies at universities abroad
such as princeton.
